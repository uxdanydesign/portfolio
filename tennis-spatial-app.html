Designing an intuitive and enjoyable VR/AR experience

2023
UX UI Designer
Apple Vision Pro

Overview

With the release of Apple Vision Pro, the opportunity to create new interactions for users led me to design an experience focused on Tennis, bringing the Set Winner app to the AR/VR world.

Goal

The goal was simple: adapt the Tennis app I had already made to the Apple Vision Pro ecosystem, ensuring that the experience is intuitive and accessible to users, without causing fatigue and generating satisfaction in them. Yes... simple.

Main challenge

How might we create an immersive Tennis viewing experience without causing user fatigue?

Process

I took my time learning all the fundamentals of VR/AR, both from Apple and elsewhere. I learned about sound design, gained a deeper understanding of motion design, and studied the accessibility rules for the platform.

Results

This helped me focus on creating an interface that maintained the essence of the app while adding valuable mechanics and features to the spatial design.
