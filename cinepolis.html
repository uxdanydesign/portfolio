Overview

While working on projects for Cinepolis (Through IA Interactive), I had the opportunity to created new solutions for the commercial app and website. In addition to created and led the design process of a suite of administrative tools for the company.
6+
Designed for six countries in the initial scope, with more to be added in the future.
100+
Collaboration with more than 100 co-workers and stakeholders from different areas and countries around the world.
6+
Positive feedback from stakeholders in different areas.

Commercial product

I participated in several War Rooms with the design team where we analyzed insights and generated new solutions. I created multiple benchmarks, detailed structures and delightful UI's for the new solutions, which were then presented to the development team for implementation.

Administrative tools

Led the design process for a suite of administrative tools aimed at optimizing processes in different areas within the company.

I had meetings with stakeholders from different countries around the world to understand their needs, problems, and workflows, which were then analyzed and converted into ideas that I discussed with the team to determine when to implement them. At the same time, different benchmarks were generated to help us find different paths and solutions.

Finally, these solutions were presented to the various stakeholders for validation and then sent for development.
