Commercial products

Helped improve the way users purchased tickets, food and tracked their preparation process. Refine various workflows and key screens to guide users, ensuring that information is transparent and easy to find.

Administrative products

Helped to implement the company’s internal processes (across various departments) within a unified system, in order to reduce the time required for each task and achieve a more streamlined workflow without relying on external touchpoints.

To achieve this, we held meetings with the teams in each department to ensure we understood their challenges, needs, and processes, and then delivered a product that is easy to understand and helps them perform their work more efficiently.
